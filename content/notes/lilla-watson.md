# Lilla Watson

Lilla Watson is an indigenous Australian artist and activist. She is known for her declaration:

> "If you have come here to help me, you are wasting your time, but if you have come because your liberation is bound up with mine, then let us work together."

In these paradigms, "we" are tightly and inextricably knotted.

For more on Lilla Watson, see: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lilla_Watson
