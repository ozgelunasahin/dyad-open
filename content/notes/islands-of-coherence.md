# Islands of Coherence in a Sea of Chaos

It took us a while to let all this sink in.

From the ache of missing real connection and true conversation, to the uneasy truth about the systemic misalignment beneath it.

So what do we do with all this? How do we move from shared discontent to real change?

We started with what we know.

The world we're in is one we built. By our decisions, by what we choose or tolerate and at what cost. This invoked us of our collective agency.

We took inspiration from many creators and thinkers before us.

> "Small islands of coherence in a sea of chaos can shift the entire system to a higher order."

...once said Ilya Prigogine. This informed us about how change happens. Change is emergent and it begins in the small. In the relational. The repeated.

So we asked ourselves: what if we created a microcosm of the world we'd rather inhabit? One not built on extraction or noise, but on genuine connection and true conversation. What if we lived it, shared it, and let it grow in the range of its resonance?

Because from where we stand, the smallest unit of coherence is two people, fully present in a conversation.

From here, we set about building [[alternatives]].
