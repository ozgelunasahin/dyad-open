# Dyad

A social sensemaking protocol for independent thinkers.

Thoughts take form in conversation.

Find companions adjacent your thinking. Meet them around your corner.

Dyad is a place for reflection and relation. An invitation to turn inward, together. To make sense of what matters to you. To find company in the quirkiest things you care about, or the hardest ones you don't know how to talk about. To get to be with another human being when you feel like it. Maybe around the corner from where you live, maybe in that city you're passing through next month.
