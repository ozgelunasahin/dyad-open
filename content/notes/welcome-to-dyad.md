# Welcome to Dyad

Dyad is a place for reflection and relation. An invitation to turn inward, together.

To make sense of what matters to you. To find company in the quirkiest things you care about, or the hardest ones you don't know how to talk about. To get to be with another human being when you feel like it. Maybe around the corner from where you live, maybe in that city you're passing through next month.

We are technologists, artists, and engineers building with the conviction that when the conditions are set with care and intention, what comes alive in us is not exploitation or distraction, but generosity, curiosity, and empathy.

This is the base we operate from. Holding ourselves to what we set out to do: Creating an alternative tool and a community where perspectives meet in conversation, in service of truth-seeking and sensemaking. Built with unwavering care for autonomy and trust.

If this feels like something you've been waiting for, it's very likely we've been waiting for you too.
