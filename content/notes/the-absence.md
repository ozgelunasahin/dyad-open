# The Kind of Absence That Stays With You

Do you remember the conversations that made you lose sense of time and space?

Not the ones where you're trying to solve something, reach a decision, or come to an agreement. The kind where you go in with an open mind, and genuine intrigue. The kind you leave feeling seen, heard, connected.

We remember. And it pained us to notice how rare they've become. That ache grew sharper once we realized it wasn't just ours, but something nearly everyone we spoke to carried. So we set out to build a way to seek and come by such conversations.

Little did we know how deep we'd have to go to uncover why truly immersive conversations have become so scarce.

What we found led us to [[calling-it-what-it-is]].
